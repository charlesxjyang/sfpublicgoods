SF Public Goods Fund

We give $1–5k grants to projects that elevate the intellectual culture of San Francisco.

Our goal is to catalyze the creation of public goods for the city of San Francisco.

Examples of public good projects

  • Telling the story of the interesting people of San Francisco in new ways
  • Public events that elevate the intellectual culture of San Francisco
  • Public art about San Francisco or other public art displays in San Francisco
  • Documenting and telling the history and story of San Francisco
  • And other projects in, for, and about the city of San Francisco

What we look for

  • Do you have demonstrated experience in doing the kind of project you're proposing? If you want to make a film, have you made films before? If you want to host an event, have you hosted events before?
  • Is this a project clearly a public good for San Francisco?

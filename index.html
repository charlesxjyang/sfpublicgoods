SF Public Goods Fund

We give $1–5k grants to projects that elevate the intellectual culture of San Francisco.

We fund the creation of public goods that are about SF or rooted in the city—things that make the people here think harder, look closer, and build better.

What we look for

  • Do you have demonstrated experience in doing the kind of project you're proposing? If you want to make a film, have you made films before? If you want to host an event, have you hosted events before?
  • Is this something that could be funded through other channels
  • Is this a project for the people or about the people of San Francisco?
